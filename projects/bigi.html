Provided QA support for 'BIGi' app, A world of content with all the kids and teenagers' series

As a QA tester for uTest's website, I was invited to perform professional tests to ensure a bug-free experience for users while playing video content from the VOD library and live stream features within the app.

Overview

'BIGi' is a content app designed for kids and teenagers. The app offers a wide range of television programs, series, movies.

The app features exclusive content from social media influencer stars and thousands of hours of content from both Israel and abroad.

With BIGi app users can enjoy the "children's channel", the "LOGI" channel, and a massive VOD library on any device, such as a smartphone, tablet, or smart TV, at any time.
