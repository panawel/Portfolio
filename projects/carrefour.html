Carrefour

In this QA job, I was invited to participate in a functional cycle for the new 'Carrefour' Website.

The main goal was to identify any core functionality issues in the shopping experience. I had the opportunity to test the Website specific on desktop environment.

E-commerce Functional Regression

Overview

Carrefour is a leading retail company that has recently launched in Israel.

The website offers a wide range of products, including groceries, household items, electronics, and more. Users can easily navigate through different categories and make online purchases.

Carrefour provides a convenient and user-friendly shopping experience, with secure payment options and reliable delivery services.
